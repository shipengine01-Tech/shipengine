What Is ShipEngine?

ShipEngine is a cloud-based warehouse and order fulfilment system designed for 3PLs, couriers, warehouses and online sellers that want simple, reliable software without complex enterprise setups. ShipEngine combines the power of a warehouse management system (WMS) with user-friendly business management software — inventory, orders, automation and billing in a single dashboard.

Warehouse Management

Real-time stock, location/bin management, receiving with ASN support and efficient put-away for faster fulfilment.

Fulfilment Automation

Auto-route orders to warehouses, auto-select courier by rules, print labels in bulk, and automatically notify customers with tracking links.

Business & Asset Management

Track warehouse assets, maintain equipment, manage staff roles and approvals, with basic HR controls for operational teams.

Client Billing

Flexible billing models — per order, per item, storage fees — and monthly statements that make accounting simple.

Frequently asked questions

What is warehouse management software?
Warehouse management software (WMS) helps businesses control inventory, receiving, put-away, picking, packing and shipping operations. ShipEngine combines WMS features with fulfilment and billing tools for 3PLs.
Does ShipEngine support multiple clients (multi-tenant)?
Yes. ShipEngine is built multi-tenant — each company has its own secure workspace, users, warehouses and billing settings.
Can I generate courier labels automatically?
Where courier APIs are available we integrate to create labels and tracking automatically. For couriers without an API, ShipEngine supports manual label upload and scanning workflows.
